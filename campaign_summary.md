# Campaign Summary

## Campaign Overview
- Multi-channel acquisition campaign across **59 qualified branches**
- Channels:
  - **Paid Social (CORE + MLO)** → scalable reach
  - **Direct Mail (3 drops)** → high-intent conversion
- Products targeted:
  - Checking (primary volume driver)
  - CD (low CPA, lower volume)
  - Mortgage New Purchase (high value)
  - Home Equity (mid-high value)

---

## Campaign Goals
- Drive **new account acquisition** across all product lines
- Generate **1,100 – 2,500+ accounts annually**
- Maintain **efficient CPA targets ($300–$650 blended)**
- Maximize **accounts per dollar spent (~$775K annual budget)**
- Support **branch-level growth across all 59 locations**
- Balance **short-term conversions (Direct Mail)** with **scalable reach (Paid Social)**

---

## Campaign Approach

### Channel Strategy
- **Paid Social**
  - Broad reach, lower CPM
  - Supports top/mid funnel
- **Direct Mail**
  - Targeted, high conversion rates
  - Drives bottom funnel performance

### Audience Strategy
- Hyperlocal targeting (branch radius + audience data)
- Product-level segmentation:
  - Checking → broad audience
  - Mortgage / HELOC → in-market audiences

### Execution Plan
- **3 Direct Mail drops annually**
- **Always-on Paid Social support**
- Budget weighted toward:
  - Checking (volume)
  - Mortgage (revenue)
